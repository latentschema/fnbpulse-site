Why restaurants lose profit

Restaurants have more data than ever, but it is scattered across multiple systems. This friction drains margins without you realizing.

Rising Food Costs

Supplier prices spike quietly. Without item-by-item auditing, your menu price stays frozen while margins disintegrate.

Hidden Waste

Over-ordering or poor prep lines mean thousands of pounds literally thrown in the bin. Spotting waste usually happens too late.

Manual Spreadsheets

Spending hours copying data from Clover into spreadsheets. By the time your Excel sheet is updated, the numbers are already historic.

Blind Profitability

Knowing your bank balance is not the same as knowing your real-time profit. Most owners wait for accountant reports 30 days after month-end.

Slow Decision Making

Without automated alerts, you notice menu under-pricing only after two consecutive quarters of negative cashflow.
